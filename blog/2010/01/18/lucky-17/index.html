Lucky 17

As I mentioned in my previous post VOTE NOW!! The SLOG had been nominated for the Top 25 VMware/Virtualisation Bloggers of the year.

As I'm sure you can guess..I came 17th!!

I'm really really happy with this, The SLOG hasn't been alive a year yet and it's been voted 17th in World and 4th in the UK. This was far more than I expected. As much as I'm proud of my achievement, I now have even more hunger to improve The SLOG and make it even more useful for all of my readers over the next year. My aim, next year…Top 10!

So I just want to say a big Thank You for anyone who voted for both me and all of my other fellow Bloggers who all work hard to make your life easier.
